Panda facts

My documentary piece aims to convey the process of hand dyeing yarns

My aim was to take the audience from start to finish of the production steps to highlight the skill and time needed to achieve this trade. My motivation for this story is to encourage others to purchase hand dyed yarn. Firstly, its unique selling point makes this story special as it will appeal to a direct niche market of those to already have knowledge of the textiles industry, therefore I aim to showcase this in an innovative way.

Stranger Facts

  • A newborn panda is about the size of a stick of butter!

Giant Pandas leave scent marks in their territories. The scent marks serve as a major form of communication. Giant Pandas can determine from the scent if another Giant Panda is in the area, if the other Giant Panda is male or female, how recently they left their mark, and, in the case of females, if they are in a reproductive period.
